A Demo of reading "Activity heart rate sharing" of Xiaomi Smart Band 6. Enable the option in official App is required.

接收小米手环6 “运动心率广播” Demo，测试前需要在小米运动健康App中开启手环的该选项。

MiBand 4 and 5 would also work.

小米手环4和5应该也可以，但我没有测试
